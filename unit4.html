How to make Frito Chili Pie

This week will be learning how to make a staple of the Texas high school football food shack.

  • Hormel Chili
  • Ground Beef
  • Frito Lays Chips
  • Shredded Cheese

First we make the chili which is nothing special due to most school trying to get the most bang for their buck

Cook a pound of ground beef and set it to the side

Put the Hormel chili in and cook it until it's nice and hot

Pour the chili onto the ground beef

Simmer for 10-15 mins

Now get the fritos, open them and pour the chili in the bag

Sprinkle shredded cheese on top

We're ready to serve it now
